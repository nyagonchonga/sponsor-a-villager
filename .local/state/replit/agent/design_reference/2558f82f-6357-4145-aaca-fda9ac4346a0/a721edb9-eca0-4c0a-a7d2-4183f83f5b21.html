How the Program Works

A comprehensive 6-step approach to transform lives and create sustainable livelihoods

1

Select Committed Villagers

Identify 4 motivated young people from rural villages who are ready to transform their lives through this opportunity.

2

NTSA Training & Licensing

Provide comprehensive bike training and obtain official NTSA licensing to ensure safety and legal compliance.

3

Transitional Housing

Secure 2 months of shared housing in Nairobi to provide stability during the transition period.

4

Electric Bike Deployment

Provide Raum Electric Bikes on deposit with comprehensive orientation and maintenance training.

5

SafeBoda Partnership

Partner with SafeBoda for platform onboarding, safety protocols, and customer service excellence training.

6

Monitor & Scale

Track progress, gather insights, and use lessons learned to scale the program to more villagers.

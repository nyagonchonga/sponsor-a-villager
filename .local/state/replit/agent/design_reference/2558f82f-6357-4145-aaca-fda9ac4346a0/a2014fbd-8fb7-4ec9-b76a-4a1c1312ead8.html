How It Works

A simple, transparent process that connects sponsors with villagers for maximum impact

1. Choose Your Villager

Browse profiles of motivated rural youth ready to transform their lives. Each profile includes their story, goals, and verification status.

2. Training & Setup

Your sponsorship covers NTSA training, housing, and electric bike deposit. We handle the logistics and ensure proper onboarding.

3. Track Progress

Stay connected through our platform. Receive regular updates, photos, and earnings reports. Watch your impact grow in real-time.
